Sailing the Macro Workshop

3rd Edition - 2023

2023 Edition

The Ph.D. in Economics of Sapienza University organizes the 3rd Sailing the Macro Workshop that will be held at the Ortygia Foundation in Ortygia, Siracusa, Italy, on 8-10 September 2023. Reflecting the breadth of modern macroeconomics, the scope of relevant topics is broad, ranging from theoretical work to empirical applications.

Invited Speakers

  • Florin Bilbiie (Cambridge University)
  • Caterina Mendicino (European Central Bank)
  • Barbara Rossi (Universitat Pompeu Fabra)
